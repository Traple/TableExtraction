Enzymatic measurements

The enzyme activity during purification was followed by NADH production with ethanol as substrate at pH 10.0 [11]. Substrate specificity was tested with aliphatic alcohols and cyclohexanol by measurements of Km and k0,, values at pH 9.9 [12].

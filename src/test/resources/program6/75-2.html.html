Attempts were made to measure the kinetic co- efﬁcients describing the oxidation of NADH by acetone. However, even with the purest acetone available to us, the progress curves for reactions were biphasic. Apparently the acetone contained some small contamination of a more-rapidly-reacting component. Because of the uncertainty introduced by the impurity, the experiments were not continued.

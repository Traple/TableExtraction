Protein extraction

Cells were harvested and washed twice with double-distilled water before being re-suspended in Y-PER detergent (Pierce, Rockford, IL, USA), 1 ml/0.6 g cells. After gentle shaking at room tem- perature for 50 min, the suspension was centrifuged for 20 min at 15 000 >< g. The supernatant was collected for enzyme puriﬁcation. Protein concen- tration was determined with the Coomassie pro- tein assay reagent (Pierce) in the Bradford assay, according to the manufacturer’s recommendations.

Alcohol dehydrogenase (Adhl) activity

NADH-dependent aldehyde reduction was deter- mined according to Wahlbom and Hahn-Hagerdal, 2002, with the following modiﬁcations: the NADH concentration was 200 uM and the substrate con- centration was 50 mM when acetaldehyde was used as substrate. All measurements were performed in biological duplicates at 300C in a U-2000 spec- trophotometer (Hitachi, Tokyo, Japan).

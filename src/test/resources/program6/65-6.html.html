Amide H/D Exchange by Mass Spectrometry. H/D ex- change was performed at lO °C with and without NAD+ (lO mM) present, and the exchange experiments and data analysis were carried out following the procedures described above. The independent activity assay confirmed that the enzyme is fully active after incubation for l5O min under the exchange conditions. A total of 21 nonoverlapping peptides generated by pepsin digestion have been identified for H/D exchange analysis (Figure 4). The H/D exchange described here only measures deuteration at backbone amides, because side-chain deuterons will back-exchange to hydrogen during LC/MS. For each peptide, the number of incorporated deuterons was obtained by calculating the difference between the weighted average mass of the peptide before and after H/D exchange. The plots of incorporated deuterons versus time were fitted with a three-exponential model to give the exchange rate constants k1, k2 and k3 and the number of corresponding exchanging hydrogens.

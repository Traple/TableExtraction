Kinetics of thermal aggregation of the preheated preparation of yADH (50 mM sodium phosphate buffer, pH 7.4, 100 mM NaCl) were studied at 56 °C by DLS. Fig. 4 shows the dependences of the light- scattering intensity on time at various concentrations of the protein in the interval from 0.025 to 0.3 mg/ml. Evidently, there is a lag period on the kinetic curves, the duration of lag period decreasing with increasing the protein concentration.

DISCUSSION

It is commonly accepted that changes of enzyme activity in the hepatocytes in the course of different liver diseases are reﬂected by increase of the corresponding

enzyme activity in the serum. Moreover, cellular injury and necrosis leads to the release of cytoplasmic enzymes. For example, the activities of aminotransferases corre- late with the severity of the condition and their monitoring is a diagnostic marker of liver cells injury during viral hepatitis (16).

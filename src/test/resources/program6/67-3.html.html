A key residue in the shuttling of the DMSO molecule toward the metal ion is S48 (Figure 2c,d). It is thought to be the residue that shuttles a proton to and from the active site. The side chain hydroxyl group of S48 is almost equidistant from the oxygen of both DMSO conformations (2.6 A for the end state and 2.7 A for the approaching conformation), as well as the metal-bound hydroxide ion WN (2.6 A).

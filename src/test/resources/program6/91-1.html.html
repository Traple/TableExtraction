Materials and methods

Strains and culture conditions

The Iﬂuyveromyces lactis strains used in this Work are listed in Table 1. Cells were grown in YP (1% yeast extract, 2% peptone) supplemented with 2% glucose (YPD), ethanol (YPE) or glycerol (YPGly) at 28 0C or in SD (yeast nitrogen base without amino acids and auxotrophic requirements) as needed. Solid media Were supplemented with 2% Bactoagar (Difco, Detroit, MI). The medium used for the selection of aar mutants was YPDEAA (YPD+0.5% ethanol and 30 mM allyl alcohol).

Mating and sporulation were performed on ME plates (5% Bacto malt extract, 2% Bacto agar; Difco). Phloxine B (Sigma, St Louis, MO) was added to YPE+agar medium, at the concentration of 2O mgL_ 1. For the triphenyl tetrazolium chloride (TTC) overlay assay, strains Were streaked on YPE plates and were allowed to grow as single colonies. A solution containing 0.5% agar (cooled at 55 OC), 0.5 M phosphate buffer (pH 7.4) and 0.1% TTC was then poured on colonies, and plates Were incubated in the dark for 10-20 min.

To change the mating type to 0c, CMK9 was crossed With MW278-20C/1 to generate the diploid strain CMK12. This diploid was then sporulated and an appropriate haploid strain, named CMK12-14D, was crossed four times with MW179-1D to obtain the isogenic strain CMK28-2A. The genetic distances, reported in Table 2, between arg, ade, thr and aar900 loci were calculated by crossing the CMK32- 12D and CMK21-50D strains.

RNA isolation and analysis

Total RNAs were prepared from exponential cultures as previously described (Schmitt et al., 1990). Ten ug of RNA from cells grown in different carbon sources were loaded onto 1.2% agarose-MOPS gels containing formaldehyde and ethidium bromide. Northern blot analysis Was per- formed by standard procedures (Sambrook et 011., 1989). The KIADH3 probe was a 600-bp SacI-Ddel fragment containing 530 nucleotides of the 5’ upstream region and 75 bp of the leader peptide-coding region (Saliola et aL, 1990). The KZADH4 probe was a 520-bp SacI-Hindlll fragment containing 475 nucleotides of the 5’ upstream region and 45 bp of the leader peptide-coding region (Saliola et al., 1991). The KZPDCI probe Was a 400-bp EcoRl/Sall DNA fragment located within the ORF (Bianchi et al., 1996). The actin probe was a 1300-bp HindIII fragment containing part of the intron and of exon 2 of the KZACTI gene (Deshler et 011., 1989).

Cell viability

After sonication, 5 uL of a cell suspension containing approximately 6 >< 106 cells mL _ 1 were placed on a thin layer of YPD agar on a microscope slide. A cover slip was placed over the cells and, after 24 h, viable and unviable cells Were scored on the basis of their ability to form microcolonies.

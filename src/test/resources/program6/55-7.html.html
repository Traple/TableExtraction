These results indicate that TADH cannot only be used to produce alcohols from ketones or aldehydes but also for the production of carboxylic acids from corresponding alde- hydes. This could open a new access to synthetically interesting carboxylic acids, by using a reaction which was not attributed to ADHs for a long time.

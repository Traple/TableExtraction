We performed Southern blot analysis to examine the copy number of ADH genes on the K. marxianus genome (Fig. 2). Total genomic DNA from K. marx- ianus was digested with BamHI, HindIII, SalI, or XbaI, which have no cleavage site inside the ADH genes, and then subjected to Southern blot analysis with approx- imately 500-bp probes corresponding to the inside regions of the four ADH ORFs. Since all four ADH genes showed high similarity in nucleotide sequence, the probes used cross-hybridized to other ADH genes. Four bands were observed for each DNA digest, though the number was not clear at high molecular weights, when BamHI was used. These results suggest that there are at least four KmADHs in K. marxianus.

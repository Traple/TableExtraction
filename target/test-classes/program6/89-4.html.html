In order to determine the pH depen- dency of the ADH activity, a mixture of buffers (buffer mix) was used for the pH range from 4.0 to 9.0 to discard different effects on activity caused by the use of independent buffers along the pH scale. The results indicated that EgADH activity exhibited a pH optimum at 7.0-7.5 (Fig. 4A).

As described in more detail elsewhere,” active-site titration of soluble and immobilized enzyme with a spin-labeled ana- log of LIO-phenanthroline (referred to as OPSL) gave the following results: 100% of the enzyme’s catalytic zinc atoms is available for binding to OPSL when LADH is in solution or immobilized by adsorption to Octyl-Sepharose; however, only 70% is available when the enzyme is immobilized by covalent attachment to CNBr-Sepharose.

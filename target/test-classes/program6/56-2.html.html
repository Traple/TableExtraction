Recombinant protein was expressed in Escherichia coli BL2l(DE3) cells (Novagen) transformed with the corresponding expression vector. Cultures were grown at 37 °C in 2 L of LB medium containing 30 ug ml-l kana- mycin. When the A600 of the culture reached 1.4, protein expression was induced by addition of isopropyl [S-D-l- thiogalactopyranoside to a concentration of 1.0 mM. The bacterial culture was incubated at 37 °C for a further 24 h.

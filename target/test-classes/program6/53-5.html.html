After IPTG induction of ADH expression in recombinant E. coli for 4 h, total, soluble, and insoluble fractions of proteins were subjected to SDS-PAGE analysis (Fig. 3). Expression levels of ADHI and ADH3 were similar to each other such as 8.4 (i0.3) and 9.6 (i0.5)% of the total protein level, respectively.

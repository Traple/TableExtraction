Polyacrylamide-gel electrophoresis

Acrylamide and bisacrylamide were puriﬁed as described by Loening (1967). All gels contained 5 "/0 (w/v) bisacrylamide. The electrophoresis and the development of alcohol dehydrogenase activity on the gels have been described in detail elsewhere (Bégin- Heick & Heick, 1970). To ensure good resolution the gels had to be used within 20min of their preparation. In some cases the gels were scanned after staining, by using the gel-scanning attachment (model 2410) of the Gilford spectrophotometer (model 2000) (Gilford Instrument Laboratories, Oberlin, Ohio, U.S.A.). i

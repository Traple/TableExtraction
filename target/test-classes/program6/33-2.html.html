SDS-PAGE and mass spectrometry

Puriﬁed fractions were run on SDS-PAGE, using a Tris—HCl 4—15% gradient precast gel and a Mini-PROTEAN 3 electrophoresis unit, both from Bio-Rad (Hercules, CA, USA). Precision Plus Protein dual colour (Bio-Rad, Hercules, CA, USA) was used as standard. The gels were stained with 0.25% Coomassie brilliant blue R- 250 (ICN Biomedicals, Aurora, OH, USA) in a 6:311 mixture of HgOzMeOHzHAc. All mass spectrometry results were obtained from SWE- GENE proteomics resource centre in Lund, Sweden (http://www.lth.se/sciblu).

Although the stimulation of ADH-dependent ethanol metabolism caused by tBOOH in our in vitro studies required rel- atively high concentrations, other alkyl hydroperox- ides such as lipid hydroperoxides might play a role in the regulation of enzymatic activity at more physiolog- ically relevant concentrations.
